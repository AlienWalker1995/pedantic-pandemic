# PM Question Bank — Product

Reference taxonomy for the `pedantic-pm` role (**Product only** — UX moved to
`ux-question-bank.md`). Prompts to adapt, not a script; every question must pass the
five-test bar in `interrogation-protocol.md`. Organizing question:
**"Why does this matter in the market?"**

## Product — why does it matter in the market?

**Problem / JTBD**
- What job is the user hiring this to do? What do they do today instead (the status quo / workaround)?
- Is this a painkiller or a vitamin? How acute is the pain?
- Whose problem is it — the user's, the buyer's, or ours?

**Target user & segment**
- Who specifically is this for? Which segment do we win first?
- Who is it explicitly *not* for in v1?
- How many such users exist, and how do we reach them?

**Value & evidence**
- What evidence do we have that users want this (requests, data, interviews)?
- What happens if we don't build it? Who churns or never converts?

**Success metrics**
- What single metric proves this worked? What's the target and by when?
- What counter-metric would tell us it's hurting something else?
- What result would make us kill or roll it back?

**Scope & MVP**
- What's the thinnest v1 that still tests the core hypothesis?
- What is explicitly cut from v1 and deferred?
- What's the riskiest assumption we should validate first/cheapest?

**Prioritization & timing**
- Why this over everything else on the roadmap right now? Why now?
- What's the cost of waiting a quarter?

**Alternatives & competition**
- How do competitors / incumbents solve this? What's our wedge or differentiation?
- Could the user solve it with an existing tool? Why won't they?

**Business model**
- Free, paid, or gated? Does it drive acquisition, activation, retention, or revenue?
- Does it change pricing, packaging, or support load?

**Non-goals & stakeholders**
- What are we deliberately NOT doing here?
- Who must sign off (RACI)? Whose roadmap does this touch?

---
UX questions (flows, states, accessibility, content) are owned by `pedantic-ux` — see
`ux-question-bank.md`. On stop, hand resolved product intent to the UX member.
